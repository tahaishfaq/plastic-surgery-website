Recovery after Lipo 360 varies by patient, treatment areas, and the surgical plan recommended by your surgeon. Swelling and bruising are common in the first weeks.

## Early recovery

Compression garments, rest, and limited activity are typically part of early care. Follow surgeon instructions rather than general timelines alone.

## Returning to activity

Light movement may resume gradually. Strenuous exercise should wait for surgeon clearance based on healing progress.

Individual results and timelines differ. This overview does not replace personalized medical advice.
