A thoughtful consultation starts with clear goals and honest questions. Before your appointment, note what you hope to improve, any prior surgeries, and medications you take regularly.

## What to bring

- A list of health conditions and allergies
- Prior surgical records if available
- Realistic questions about recovery, scars, and activity limits

## Questions worth asking

Ask how the surgeon assesses candidacy, what recovery looks like week by week, and how results may vary based on your anatomy.
