Traveling for surgery requires coordination beyond the procedure itself. Plan your visit around consultation timing, recovery needs, and follow-up availability.

## Before you travel

Confirm consultation format, required medical records, and expected length of stay. Allow buffer days for swelling management and post-operative reviews.

## During recovery

Choose accommodation that supports rest and easy clinic access. Keep emergency contacts and surgeon instructions within reach.

Our coordination team can help outline a provisional plan. Final travel guidance is confirmed during consultation.
